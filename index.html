Something About Me

I am Renzo Xavier, a psychologist graduated from the University of the Republic with a cognitive-behavioral orientation. This background has given me a broad perspective on artificial intelligence, user experience, data analysis, and machine learning.

In addition, I am a programming analyst with experience in several programming languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, SQL Server, and MySQL, as well as modern frameworks like Ionic and React. I also have expertise in software design and in implementing team collaboration strategies, both in remote environments —using Git— and through agile methodologies such as Scrum.

I am passionate about web development, both frontend and backend. I enjoy tackling challenges, solving complex problems, and working on projects that aim to make an impact. Beyond programming, I teach computer science, explore new technologies, and contribute to developer communities. Recently, I have been delving into data science and machine learning, fields that greatly excite me for their potential.

Currently, I am pursuing a Diploma in Data Business Analytics and participating in the Jóvenes a Programar program. My English level is A2–B1, and I am highly motivated and enthusiastic about learning.
